<h1>DashTracker Privacy Policy</h1>
<p><strong>Last updated: March 28, 2026</strong></p>

<p>DashTracker is a 100% offline app. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.</p>

<h2>What data does the app collect?</h2>
<p><strong>None.</strong></p>
<p>All your shifts, expenses, receipts, photos, and Delivery Proofs are stored <strong>only on your device</strong>.</p>

<h2>Permissions explained</h2>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Location</strong> — Used only for real-time GPS mileage tracking during a dash. Data never leaves your phone.</li>
    <li><strong>Camera</strong> — Used only for scanning receipts and taking Delivery Proof photos. Photos stay on your device.</li>
    <li><strong>Storage</strong> — Used to save your backups, receipts, and Delivery Proofs locally.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Google Drive Backup (optional)</h2>
<p>If you choose to back up to Google Drive, the app will only send the ZIP file you explicitly create. You control this completely.</p>

<h2>No ads • No analytics • No tracking</h2>
<p>DashTracker contains no advertising, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party tracking of any kind.</p>

<p>Your data never leaves your phone unless <strong>you</strong> choose to export or back it up.</p>

<p>If you have any questions, feel free to email me at <strong>through the play store</strong>.</p>

<p>Thank you for using DashTracker!</p>
